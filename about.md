# About Global Alpha Engine

![Multi-Agent Hedge Fund Banner](/assets/images/banner.svg)

## What is This?

Global Alpha Engine is an autonomous algorithmic trading system that uses a **collaborative multi-agent architecture** to make trading decisions. Instead of a single monolithic algorithm, four specialized AI agents work together:

1. **The Analyst** — Processes news, earnings, and sentiment data
2. **The Quant** — Develops and backtests mathematical trading strategies  
3. **The Risk Manager** — Ensures capital preservation and enforces trading limits
4. **The Librarian** — Manages data integrity and point-in-time datasets

## Why Multi-Agent?

Most trading bots fail because they:
- Overfit to historical data
- Lack risk management discipline
- Can't explain their decisions
- Have no checks and balances

Our multi-agent design mirrors real hedge fund structure—each agent specializes and checks the others' work.

## Technology Stack

- **Python 3.9+**
- **yfinance** — Market data
- **pandas/numpy** — Data processing
- **bt** — Backtesting engine
- **quantstats** — Performance analytics
